About Us

Welcome to civilpal, your one-step stop for all things government-related. Our platform connects citizens with government services, making it easier to report issues, track resolutions, and access important information.

We are committed to transparency, efficiency, and citizen engagement in government operations. Our portal serves as a bridge between the public and various government departments.

Our Services

Issue Reporting

Report problems in your area including infrastructure issues, public service complaints, and more.

Application Tracking

Track the status of your government applications and requests in real-time.

Document Access

Access important government documents, forms, and public records.

Service Requests

Request government services like birth certificates, licenses, and permits.

Issues Resolved

Report and track issues related to public services, infrastructure, and more. We are committed to resolving your concerns promptly.

Our system automatically routes your complaints to the appropriate department and provides real-time updates on resolution progress. You can view statistics on resolved issues in your constituency and compare response times across different departments.

Status Tracking

Check the status of your requests, applications, or complaints. Stay informed about the progress of your submissions.

Our tracking system provides detailed information about each stage of processing, estimated completion times, and contact information for the department handling your case.
